Q-Her Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Summary: Q-Her is a dating app that connects people through trusted intermediaries called "Cupids". We collect only the data necessary to provide our services and never sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, interests, city, country
  • Dating Preferences: Age range, gender preferences, location preferences
  • Communications: Messages you send to other users

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Last login time, app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

1.3 Location Information

  • Declared Location: City and country you provide in your profile
  • Precise Location: Only used for Emergency SOS feature (with your explicit consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with potential partners based on your preferences
  • To enable communication between users
  • To provide the Cupid recommendation system
  • To send important service notifications
  • To ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • To provide Emergency SOS alerts to your emergency contacts

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photos, bio, city) is visible to other users. You can control visibility of certain data (age, last active status) in Privacy Settings.

3.2 With Cupids

If you choose a Cupid, they can see your profile and preferences to make recommendations on your behalf.

3.3 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data for advertising purposes
  • Use tracking or analytics that identify you personally

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Access to user data is restricted and logged

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Chat messages: Retained for 12 months after last activity

7. Age Restriction

Q-Her is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@q-her.com
  • Support: support@q-her.com
